Data Security

Your Privacy & Data Security

How We Protect Your Information

Minago is built around a simple principle: your most sensitive information should be readable only by you — not by us, not by our team, and not by anyone else.

What Information We Store

We store the details you add about your belongings and properties so you can access them from any device after signing in. This includes names, photos, categories, and other details you choose to add.

Certain information — such as addresses, financial values, insurance policy numbers, and other sensitive details — is treated with a higher level of protection than the rest of your data.

How We Protect Your Sensitive Information

Your sensitive information is encrypted before it is stored. This means that even if someone were to access our database directly, they would see only unreadable data — not your actual values, addresses, or policy numbers.

The key used to decrypt your information is never stored on your device and never stored by us in readable form. It is only made available to you when you are actively signed in to your account.

Your Account and Your Data

Your data is linked to your account, not to any specific device or set of login credentials. This means:

  • You can access your information from any device by signing in
  • Changing your email, password, or display name does not affect your access to your data
  • Signing in on a new phone works the same as on your current one

What We Cannot See

Our team does not have access to your sensitive information in readable form. This includes:

  • Exact addresses and locations
  • Purchase prices and estimated values
  • Insurance policy numbers and policyholder details
  • Mortgage information
  • Any other financial or personal details you have entered

Access Logging

Every time sensitive information is accessed within the app, a record is created in your account. This log is for your protection and helps ensure that your data is only accessed by you.

Your Responsibility

Your data is protected as long as your account is secure. We recommend using a strong, unique password and enabling any additional security options offered by your email provider.

If you believe your account has been compromised, change your password immediately.

Contact

For questions about your data or to request deletion of your account and all associated information, please contact us.
