Game

Amazeballz

A physics‑driven tilt puzzler where precision beats speed. Roll, spin, and time your moves to guide color‑matched balls into their holes on a sleek, minimalist board.

Download on the App Store

How It Works

Tilt your device to roll the balls across the board. Match each ball to its color‑coded hole. Sounds simple — but gravity, momentum, and obstacles make every level a fresh challenge. No timers, no pressure — just you, the board, and physics.

Features

Real‑World Physics Every tilt, spin, and bounce feels authentic — best experienced on a real device.
Minimalist Design Clean boards, bold colors, zero clutter. The focus is entirely on the puzzle.
Precision Over Speed No countdown clocks. Take your time, plan your path, and nail the perfect roll.
Tactile Gameplay Designed for hands‑on play. Feel every subtle motion as the balls glide and collide.
