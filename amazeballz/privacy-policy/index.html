Amazeballz Privacy Policy

Urban Army AB ("we", "us", or "our") built Amazeballz as a free app. This page explains how we handle your data — the short version is: we don't collect any.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Amazeballz is a fully offline game. It does not require an internet connection, user accounts, or login credentials.

Device Sensors

The app accesses your device's accelerometer and gyroscope solely to control gameplay (tilting and spinning the phone to roll balls). Sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Amazeballz does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Amazeballz does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children. Because no personal information is collected from any user, no special data handling is required for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Data Storage

Game progress (such as fastest time and win count) is stored locally on your device only. This data is not accessible to us or any third party and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
