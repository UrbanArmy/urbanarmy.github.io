Account Deletion

Delete Your Nappet Account

To request deletion of your Nappet account and associated data, please follow the steps below. We will process your request within 30 days.

Note: If you want to delete individual pieces of data, please visit our Data Deletion page instead.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to nappet@urbanarmy.se with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address in the message
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days
  4. Your account and data will be deleted within 30 days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

Important Notes

  • This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
  • If you want to use Nappet again, you'll need to create a new account.
  • Content you shared publicly may have been copied by other users before deletion.
