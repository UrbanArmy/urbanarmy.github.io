How It Works

Join live fishing contests, register your catches with photos, and follow the leaderboard in real time. Compete with friends and other anglers, track your progress, and celebrate your victories.

Features

Live Leaderboard Real-time standings for every contest. Watch results update instantly as catches are registered — no refresh needed.
Photo Catches Document every fish with a photo. Register weight in grams and see catches with images on the leaderboard.
Easy Contest Setup Create a competition in seconds. Add participants, assign start numbers, and manage everything from your phone.
Open for Everyone Spectators follow along without an account. Contestants sign up digitally — no paper lists or spreadsheets.
