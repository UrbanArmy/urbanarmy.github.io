Data Deletion

Delete Your Tilit Account

To request deletion of your Tilit account and associated data, please follow the steps below. We will process your request within 30 days.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to tilit@urbanarmy.se with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address in the message
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request within 3 business days
  4. Your account and data will be deleted within 30 days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, bio, profile photo)
  • Your restaurant recommendations and personal notes
  • Your tags and taste profile settings
  • Your wishlists and bookmarked places
  • Your follower and following connections
  • Your lists and collections
  • Your account settings and preferences

What We Retain

For legal compliance and operational purposes, we may retain the following for a limited time:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (retained for 30 days)
  • Transaction logs required by law (retained as legally required)
  • Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you personally

Important Notes

  • This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
  • If you want to use Tilit again, you'll need to create a new account.
  • Content you shared publicly may have been copied by other users before deletion.
