Data Deletion

Delete Your Data from Tilit

This page explains how to delete individual pieces of data from Tilit while keeping your account active. You can remove recommendations, photos, lists, or other content you've created.

Note: If you want to delete your entire account and all data, please visit our Account Deletion page instead.

What Data Can You Delete?

  • Recommendations: Individual restaurant reviews, notes, comments, ratings, tags, and photos
  • Lists & Collections: Custom restaurant lists, wishlists, bookmarks, and saved places
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, display name, and location settings
  • Social Data: Follower connections, following lists, shared content, and activity history

How to Delete Specific Data

Option 1: Delete Items Within the App

  1. Open the Tilit app
  2. Navigate to the content you want to delete:
    • Recommendations: Profile → Recommendations → Tap recommendation → Menu (⋮) → Delete
    • Photos: Open recommendation or place → Tap photo → Delete icon
    • Lists: Lists tab → Tap list → Menu (⋮) → Delete List
    • Bookmarks: Bookmarks → Swipe left on item → Delete
    • Profile info: Profile → Edit Profile → Remove or change information
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted
  4. The data will be deleted immediately

Option 2: Request Bulk Data Deletion via Email

If you want to delete multiple items or specific types of data at once:

  1. Send an email to tilit@urbanarmy.se with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address and username
  3. Specify what type of data you want to delete (e.g., "all my restaurant recommendations in Stockholm" or "all photos from 2025")
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once the data has been deleted

Important Information

  • Deletion is permanent. Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
  • Your account remains active. Deleting specific data does not close your account.
  • Shared content: If others have bookmarked or interacted with your content, your content will be removed from their views as well.
  • Backup systems: Deleted data may persist in backup systems for up to 30 days before complete removal.

Data Retention After Deletion

When you delete specific data:

  • Immediate removal: The data is immediately removed from the app and our primary database
  • Backup systems: Data may remain in backup systems for up to 30 days before permanent deletion
  • Anonymized analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (without personal identifiers) may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal requirements: Any data we're legally required to retain will be kept only for the minimum required period

Need to Delete Your Entire Account?

If you want to completely close your account and delete all associated data, visit our Account Deletion page.
