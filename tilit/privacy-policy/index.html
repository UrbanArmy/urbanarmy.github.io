Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Urban Army AB ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Tilit mobile application. This page explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name and username
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Date of birth
  • Gender (optional)
  • Home city and country (optional)
  • Biography (optional)

User-Generated Content

We store content you create in the app:

  • Restaurant recommendations and notes
  • Personal tags and taste preferences
  • Lists and collections
  • Wishlists and bookmarks
  • Follow relationships and social connections

Location Information

We collect approximate location data (city and country) that you provide when setting your home location or when browsing restaurants. We do not track your precise GPS location.

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Search queries
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and error logs

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Tilit service
  • Enable social features (following, recommendations from friends)
  • Personalize your experience and show relevant content
  • Send notifications about activity on your account (if enabled)
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Public Information

If your profile is public, the following information is visible to other users:

  • Your username, display name, and profile photo
  • Your restaurant recommendations (unless marked private)
  • Your public lists
  • Your followers and following list

Service Providers

We use third-party services to operate Tilit:

  • Firebase (Google) – Authentication, database, file storage, and cloud functions
  • Google Maps – Location and mapping services

These providers may have access to your data to perform services on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data stored in Firebase is encrypted at rest
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth with secure password hashing
  • Access to backend systems is restricted and logged

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal compliance.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Control your privacy settings (public vs. private profile)
  • Opt out of notifications

To exercise these rights, contact us at tilit@urbanarmy.se or use the in-app settings.

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. See our Account Deletion page for instructions.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time. See our Data Deletion page for instructions.

Children's Privacy

Tilit is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States (Firebase/Google Cloud). By using Tilit, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the app or via email. Your continued use of Tilit after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
