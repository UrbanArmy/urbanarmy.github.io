How It Works

Recommend your favorite restaurants, add personal tags and notes, and see where your friends eat. Follow chefs, food writers, and curators whose taste you trust. No algorithms pushing sponsored listings — just real picks from real people.

Features

Trusted Recommendations See where friends and friends‑of‑friends eat. Follow curators, chefs, and food lovers you trust.
Personal Tags & Notes Tag experiences by vibe, occasion, and price. Add private notes to remember what made it special.
Curated Database Browse quality‑controlled restaurants worldwide. No spam, no fake reviews.
Social Discovery Create and share restaurant lists. Build your wishlist. Find great meals, anywhere.
